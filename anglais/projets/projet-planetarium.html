Application for the Strasbourg Planetarium

2023 - UI/UX Design - Université de Strasbourg

The user scrolls down the application and explores the different scales of the universe. This project offers visitors a new approach before visiting the Strasbourg planetarium. Its ambition is to offer a narrative, in the image of the planetarium, and a journey through the universe.

The project's main challenge was to offer a narrative application that was fun and accessible to all. It was therefore necessary to offer a tutorial at launch.

Website designed in HTML/CSS and JavaScript
Alessandro Bocquet.
