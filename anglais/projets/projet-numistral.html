Redesign Numistral

2024 - UI/UX Design - Université de Strasbourg

This project is a redesign of the Numistral website, with the aim of improving navigation and archive searching. The first step was to examine the strengths and weaknesses of the original site.

It has been designed to maintain a uniform user interface. As the aim of the site is to bring together all the archive resources of different organizations, it is important to establish a standard for each archive and an index.

Overhauling the archive sorting and retrieval tool was a key stage in this project.

Website designed in HTML/CSS and JavaScript
Alessandro Bocquet.
