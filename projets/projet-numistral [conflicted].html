Redesign Numistral

2024 - UI/UX Design - Université de Strasbourg

This project is an overhaul of the Numistral website, with the aim of improving navigation and archive searches. The first step was to consider the shortcomings and strengths of the original website.

It has been designed to maintain a uniform UI. As the aim of the site is to bring together all the archive resources of different bodies, it is important to establish a standard for each archive and an index.

Overhauling the archive sorting and retrieval tool was a key stage in this project.

Website realised with HTML/CSS and JavaScript
