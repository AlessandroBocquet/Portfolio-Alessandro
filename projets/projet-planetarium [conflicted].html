App for the Planétarium de Strasbourg

2023 - UI/UX Design - Université de Strasbourg

The user scrolls down on the app and explores the scales of the universe. This project offers visitors a new approach to visiting the planetarium. Its ambition is to offer a narrative, just like the planetarium, and a journey through the universe.

The main challenge of the project was to offer an application that was narrative, fun and accessible to everyone. It was therefore necessary to offer a tutorial at the launch.

Website realised with HTML/CSS and JavaScript
