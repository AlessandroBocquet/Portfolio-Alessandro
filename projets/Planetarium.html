Planetarium of Strasbourg

UI/UX Design · Branding
Description

Planetarium of Strasbourg is a digital experience project that combines astronomical education with interactive design. The project focuses on creating an engaging interface that helps visitors explore the cosmos through modern digital tools.

Problem:

Traditional planetarium experiences often lack interactivity and fail to engage modern audiences. Visitors need a more intuitive way to explore astronomical content and understand complex scientific concepts.

Solution :

We designed an interactive digital experience that combines beautiful visualizations with intuitive navigation. The interface guides users through astronomical content while maintaining educational clarity and aesthetic appeal.
